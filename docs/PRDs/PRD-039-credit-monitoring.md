# PRD-039 — Credit Score Monitoring

**Priority**: P3
**Status**: Draft
**Registry**: [REGISTRY.md](./REGISTRY.md)

---

## Problem Statement

Credit scores affect loan rates, apartment applications, and insurance premiums, but most people only check when they need credit. Continuous monitoring with AI-powered improvement recommendations helps users build and maintain good credit proactively.

---

## Key Ideas

- Score tracking with trend visualization over time
- Factor breakdown (utilization, payment history, age, mix, inquiries)
- Improvement simulations ("if you pay down $500, score goes up ~X")
- Hard inquiry and new account alerts
- Utilization optimization recommendations
- Score impact predictions for planned financial decisions
- Dispute assistance for inaccurate items
- Goal-based tracking (e.g., "get to 750 by December")

---

## Success Metrics

- Credit monitoring enrollment rate (% of eligible users)
- Average score improvement over 6 and 12 months
- Recommendation action rate (% of suggestions followed)
- Alert response time (how quickly users act on alerts)
- User-reported credit confidence score
