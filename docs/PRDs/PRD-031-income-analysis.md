# PRD-031 — Income Pattern Analysis

**Priority**: P2
**Status**: Draft
**Registry**: [REGISTRY.md](./REGISTRY.md)

---

## Problem Statement

36% of Americans do gig or freelance work with variable income. Traditional finance tools assume steady paychecks. The agent should understand income patterns — regular, irregular, seasonal, multi-source — and adapt recommendations accordingly.

---

## Key Ideas

- Automatic income source detection and labeling
- Regularity scoring per income stream (steady, variable, seasonal)
- Income prediction for variable earners based on historical patterns
- Feast/famine planning with buffer recommendations
- Tax set-aside recommendations for 1099 income
- Income diversification insights and risk assessment
- Income trend tracking with year-over-year comparisons

---

## Success Metrics

- Income source detection accuracy (% correctly identified)
- Prediction accuracy for next-month income (variable earners)
- User-reported confidence in financial planning
- Tax set-aside adoption rate among 1099 users
- Reduction in overdraft/shortfall events for variable-income users
