# PRD-021 — Round-Up Savings to Yield

**Priority**: P2
**Status**: Draft
**Registry**: [REGISTRY.md](./REGISTRY.md)

---

## Problem Statement

Saving money feels hard when done in large chunks, causing most people to save inconsistently or not at all. Micro-saving by rounding up purchases to the nearest dollar is painless and adds up quickly. Combined with yield-bearing positions, round-ups become an invisible savings engine that grows automatically.

---

## Key Ideas

- Configurable round-up multipliers (1x, 2x, 5x the round-up amount)
- Daily batch sweeps from checking to yield-bearing account
- Automatic yield generation on accumulated round-ups
- Monthly round-up summary showing total saved and yield earned
- Pause/resume controls for tight months
- Projected annual savings calculator
- Milestone rewards and savings streak tracking

---

## Success Metrics

- Average monthly round-up savings per user
- Round-up feature activation and retention rate
- Yield earned on round-up balances
- Pause/resume frequency (proxy for flexibility value)
- Incremental savings (round-ups that wouldn't have been saved otherwise)
