# PRD-022 — Bill Calendar & Payment Scheduling

**Priority**: P2
**Status**: Draft
**Registry**: [REGISTRY.md](./REGISTRY.md)

---

## Problem Statement

Users can't visualize their payment obligations over time. Lists of bills and due dates don't convey the temporal rhythm of financial life. A calendar view of all upcoming bills, subscriptions, and expected expenses helps users plan cash flow and avoid surprises. Visual is better than tabular for temporal planning.

---

## Key Ideas

- Monthly and weekly calendar view of all obligations
- Drag-to-reschedule payment dates where possible
- Income overlay showing when money comes in vs. goes out
- Cash flow forecast line (running balance projection)
- Overdue and at-risk payment highlighting
- Recurring pattern visualization (weekly, monthly, annual)
- Export to external calendars (Google, Apple, Outlook)

---

## Success Metrics

- Calendar view adoption rate among active users
- Late payment reduction after calendar activation
- Cash flow surprise reduction (unplanned overdrafts or shortfalls)
- Calendar export usage
- User engagement frequency with calendar view
