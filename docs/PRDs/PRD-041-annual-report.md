# PRD-041 — Annual Financial Report

**Priority**: P3
**Status**: Draft
**Registry**: [REGISTRY.md](./REGISTRY.md)

---

## Problem Statement

Year-end is when people reflect on finances and set goals. A comprehensive, beautifully designed annual report showing spending patterns, savings achieved, agent ROI, and year-over-year trends creates a powerful engagement and retention moment.

---

## Key Ideas

- Spotify Wrapped-style annual financial summary
- Total saved by agent and yield earned over the year
- Bills optimized and subscriptions cancelled recap
- Spending category breakdown with surprises highlighted
- Biggest wins and most impactful agent actions
- Year-over-year comparison for returning users
- Shareable highlights with privacy-safe formatting
- Goal-setting prompts for the upcoming year
- Tax-ready export of relevant financial data

---

## Success Metrics

- Report view rate (% of eligible users who open their report)
- Share rate (% who share highlights on social or with friends)
- Post-report goal-setting rate (% who set a new goal)
- January retention lift vs. users who didn't view the report
- Viral acquisition (new signups attributed to shared reports)
