# PRD-023 — Weekly Financial Digest

**Priority**: P2
**Status**: Draft
**Registry**: [REGISTRY.md](./REGISTRY.md)

---

## Problem Statement

Not everyone opens the app daily, but everyone should stay informed about their financial health. Users who disengage miss agent actions, upcoming bills, and savings opportunities. A weekly digest email summarizing key metrics and agent activity keeps users engaged without requiring app opens.

---

## Key Ideas

- Personalized email digest with key metrics (spent, saved, earned yield)
- Agent action summary (what the agent did on your behalf this week)
- Upcoming bills and payment reminders
- Savings opportunities identified but not yet acted on
- Week-over-week spending trends and highlights
- One-tap actions directly from the email (approve, dismiss, snooze)
- Configurable frequency: daily, weekly, or monthly

---

## Success Metrics

- Digest open rate and click-through rate
- Re-engagement rate (users who return to app from digest)
- Action completion rate from digest CTAs
- Unsubscribe rate (proxy for perceived value)
- Retention lift for digest subscribers vs. non-subscribers
