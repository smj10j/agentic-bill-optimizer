# PRD-017 — Deal Scout & Cashback Finder

**Priority**: P1
**Status**: Draft
**Registry**: [REGISTRY.md](./REGISTRY.md)

---

## Problem Statement

Users miss savings opportunities because they don't know about cashback offers, better rates, or promotions relevant to their spending. Deals exist but are scattered across dozens of platforms. The agent should proactively surface deals based on actual spending patterns — not generic coupon spam.

---

## Key Ideas

- Spending-pattern-based deal matching (suggest deals for merchants you actually use)
- Cashback aggregation across card programs and platforms
- Price comparison for recurring purchases (groceries, gas, subscriptions)
- Coupon and promo code surfacing at point of decision
- Loyalty program optimization (are you maximizing your points?)
- Seasonal and time-sensitive deal alerts
- Estimated annual savings dashboard

---

## Success Metrics

- Dollars saved per user per month via surfaced deals
- Deal engagement rate (deals acted on / deals shown)
- Cashback captured vs. cashback available
- User retention lift from deal engagement
- Net new savings identified per active user
