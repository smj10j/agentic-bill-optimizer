# PRD-032 — Smart Receipt Scanner

**Priority**: P2
**Status**: Draft
**Registry**: [REGISTRY.md](./REGISTRY.md)

---

## Problem Statement

Not all spending is captured digitally. Cash purchases, reimbursable expenses, and split bills often go untracked. A receipt scanner that auto-categorizes and integrates with the financial picture gives a more complete view.

---

## Key Ideas

- Camera/photo OCR for receipt capture
- Auto-categorization of line items and totals
- Merchant detection and matching to known vendors
- Tax-deductible expense flagging
- Reimbursement tracking with status workflow
- Warranty extraction and storage from receipts
- Return window tracking with countdown alerts
- Receipt archive with full-text search

---

## Success Metrics

- OCR accuracy (% of receipts parsed correctly without manual edits)
- Receipts scanned per user per month
- Spending coverage improvement (% of total spend now tracked)
- Reimbursement recovery rate ($ claimed that would have been missed)
- User satisfaction with categorization accuracy
