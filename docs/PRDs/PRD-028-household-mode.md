# PRD-028 — Family & Household Mode

**Priority**: P2
**Status**: Draft
**Registry**: [REGISTRY.md](./REGISTRY.md)

---

## Problem Statement

Finances are rarely solo. Couples, families, and roommates share expenses, bills, and financial goals. Current single-user design forces households to either merge everything or manage separately with no coordination. Orbit should support shared views, split responsibilities, and collaborative financial management without requiring everyone to merge their entire financial lives.

---

## Key Ideas

- Household creation and member invites with role permissions
- Shared vs. personal views (toggle between household and individual)
- Bill assignment and responsibility splitting
- Shared goals with individual contribution tracking
- Spending transparency controls (what each member can see)
- Household-level insights and aggregate reporting
- Split expense tracking and settlement
- Allowance and kids mode for financial literacy

---

## Success Metrics

- Household creation and invite acceptance rate
- Multi-member active household retention vs. single-user retention
- Shared goal completion rate
- Bill splitting accuracy and settlement speed
- Household NPS vs. individual user NPS
