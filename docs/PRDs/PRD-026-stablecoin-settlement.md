# PRD-026 — Stablecoin Settlement Layer

**Priority**: P2
**Status**: Draft
**Registry**: [REGISTRY.md](./REGISTRY.md)

---

## Problem Statement

Traditional payment rails are slow (ACH takes 2-3 days), expensive (wire fees, interchange), and opaque. Stablecoin settlement enables instant, low-cost money movement — but users should never need to know it's happening. This is an invisible infrastructure upgrade that makes payments faster and cheaper behind the scenes.

---

## Key Ideas

- USDC mint/redeem integration for seamless fiat conversion
- Instant settlement for bill payments (no more 2-3 day waits)
- Yield generation via DeFi protocols (invisible to user, shown as APY)
- Fiat on/off ramps with minimal friction
- Transaction cost comparison (traditional rails vs. stablecoin)
- Compliance and regulatory framework for money transmission
- Automatic fallback to traditional rails when needed

---

## Success Metrics

- Settlement time reduction (average hours saved per transaction)
- Transaction cost savings vs. traditional rails
- Yield improvement from DeFi vs. traditional savings
- Fallback rate (how often traditional rails are needed)
- Regulatory compliance audit pass rate
