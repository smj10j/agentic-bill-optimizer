# PRD-018 — Debt Paydown Optimizer

**Priority**: P1
**Status**: Draft
**Registry**: [REGISTRY.md](./REGISTRY.md)

---

## Problem Statement

77% of American households carry debt, yet most people don't know the mathematically optimal paydown strategy. The difference between avalanche (highest rate first) and snowball (smallest balance first) can be thousands of dollars — but motivation matters too. The agent should create and execute an optimal debt repayment plan that balances math with behavioral psychology.

---

## Key Ideas

- Debt inventory: balances, interest rates, minimum payments, due dates
- Avalanche, snowball, and hybrid strategy recommendations
- Extra payment allocation engine (where does the next dollar go?)
- Payoff date projections per strategy with interest saved comparisons
- Refinance and balance transfer opportunity alerts
- Debt-free countdown timer and motivational milestones
- Automatic extra payment execution (with approval)

---

## Success Metrics

- Total interest saved vs. minimum-payment baseline
- Months shaved off payoff timeline
- User adherence to recommended plan (% of suggested payments made)
- Refinance opportunities surfaced and acted on
- Debt-free milestone completion rate
